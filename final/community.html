RICKER'S IS WORKING TO IMPROVE INDIANA'S QUALITY OF LIFE.

Jay & Nancy Ricker are extremely involved in the Anderson and Madison County community. Jay serves on the board of the Madison County Community Foundation. Jay is also the past president of the Chamber of Commerce and past chair of the Anderson Community Hospital board. Nancy is the president of the local Habitat for Humanity affiliate and serves on the board of the Anderson Symphony Orchestra. Jay and Nancy are also extremely involved in the convenience store trade association, serving as chairperson of various committees and on various boards as well as with BP and Marathon.

Alternatives, Inc.

Alternatives, Inc. is a project that Ricker Oil Company, Inc. became involved in this past year through its Safe Harbor Program. There are eight Anderson Ricker’s involved in this program. Additionally, our corporate staff has been involved in donating supplies and items to Alternatives for the past several years as a commitment and belief in the program.

Donations

Thank you for your interest in Ricker Oil Company. At Ricker’s, we make every effort to give back to the communities that have supported us since we started our business in 1979. Due to the number of donation and sponsorship requests we receive each day to support numerous and worthwhile causes, we have established the following Donation Guidelines to help better manage them. It is our hope that these guidelines will help us make the largest impact on our communities as possible.

Unfortunately, we are unable to give on an individual basis, but we are happy to consider your request if your group or organization meets the following criteria:

  • You are a local, non-profit organization serving a community in which there is a Ricker’s convenience store
  • You have a need-based request for funding or support

Please include all pertinent information with your request:

  • Name of organization
  • Website (if applicable)
  • Main contact
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • All relevant event information
Please submit your request to: donations@rickers.net.
You may also mail your request to:

Donations at Ricker Oil Co.
PO Box 150
Anderson, Indiana 46016-0150

All requests will be given consideration, and you will be notified of our decision within 30 days of receipt.
